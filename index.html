Lessonity

Make and take great lessons

With Lessonity, you can now take and make lessons across categories such as Marketing & Commerce, Languages, Photography, Programming and Computers, Art, Craft and Design, DIY, and Sciences . Highlighting the ability of sharing lessons in the app is the feature of an in app currency system. With the currency, you can take more lessons. You can obtain the currency by making a great lesson which people take. Alongside this feature is one to help style your lessons:- Markdown. Providing a great user interface is a goal of every app, and so it is with this app too. The interface doesn't get in your way and makes sure you can be comfortable without having to sacrifice any features, with scrollable text, big fonts and distinctive buttons.
