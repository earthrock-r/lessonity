Guidelines for making a lessons

  • Keep the content for a byte small and relevant.
  • Keep relevant titles and do not clickbait.
  • Make sure the lesson doesn't have irrelevant links.
  • To make your lesson enjoyable, try not to use way too much of Markdown.
  • Don't make unnecessary or test lessons; they are not accepted and can be removed.
  • Use your own ideas, we know that everyone has their own ideas and you should bring yours live, not copy others.
  • Use relevant images. Try not to use copyrighted images. Even if you do so, please credit the source.

Markdown

Italics: *italics* or _italics_
Bold:-**bold** or __bold__
Headers:-
# Header 1
## Header 2
### Header 3
#### Header 4
##### Header 5
###### Header 6
 Code:- `code`
Links:- [Links](www.example.com/)
